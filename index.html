About electronics and communication engineering

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering was started in the year 1968. Currently the department offers undergraduate, post graduate and research programmes. Our department has dynamic and vibrant faculty specialized in various fields of Electronics and Communication Engineering with proven research experience in niche areas. The department has produced 20 doctorates in the various area. The faculty members of the department have undertaken innovative projects from leading agencies such as AICTE, UGC, DST. our curriculum ensures that strong foundation in the core domain is laid in the students, paving the way for employment in prestigious multi-national companies and organizations in Core sector and in diversified fields. Our laboratories are equipped with state-of- the-art equipment. The practical courses deliver hands-on experience to students based on the changing needs of society. Our department prides in providing well balanced tutor-ward relationship, active alumni network, excellent placement opportunities to the students and consultancy to the leading industries.

About PINNACLE

Our National level technical symposium, Pinnacle is a dynamic platform where professionals, researchers, and students converge to share and explore the latest advancements in science, engineering, and technology. These events typically feature keynote speakers, paper presentations, workshops, and discussions, fostering an environment for the exchange of ideas and knowledge. Attendees have the opportunity to delve into cutting-edge research, engage in networking, and gain insights into emerging trends, making technical symposiums vital in promoting collaboration and innovation within various fields of study.

Code of conduct

  • All participates can register through our college website and even on-spot registration is available.
  • Registration for non technical events will be only through our college website and the events will be conducted online.
  • Dates for non technical events will be informed prior to participants their respective mail id's.
  • Each participants will be give separate id cards for events participation.
  • Only participants with ID card will be permitted for participating in the events.
  • For group based technical events ,a maximum of 4 members in a team is allowed.
  • Certificates for technical events winners will be given after the event.
  • For outside participants accommodation with food will be available ,which will be charged separately.
  • Participants should give prior information to the event coodinators for accomodation.
